About Me

I am a dynamic professional with a diverse background in law and a strong passion for embracing new challenges. Originally from São Paulo, Brazil, I earned my Law degree in 2005 and have over 16 years of experience as an attorney. In 2007, I deepened my knowledge of Law when I graduated with a postgraduate degree in Public Law, which qualified me for a teaching role in higher education. Later, I pursued a second postgraduate degree in Business Law.

I have also written several legal articles for "Diário de Comércio e Indústria", a Brazilian newspaper based in the state of São Paulo. My career has been enriched by a deep engagement in the arts as hobbies, including music, writing, modeling, and theater. I have participated in creative courses and events, such as theater at "Teatro Escola Macunaíma", and have played roles in theatrical productions as well as a photography project. I have been fortunate to travel to various countries, experiences that have broadened my perspective. I have lived in São Paulo, spent a brief period in Sydney, and now reside in Porto. My travels have allowed me to explore and immerse myself in different cultures across countries like the USA, Mexico, Jamaica, several in Latin America, Sweden, Portugal, and others.

In 2022, I relocated to Portugal, obtaining legal registration to continue my legal career. However, in 2024, fueled by a desire for growth and creativity, I made a significant career shift to Information Technology. Currently, I am developing skills to become a UX/UI Developer, focusing on design techniques and usability, web multimedia tools, web creation, editing and prototyping, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, content management systems, UI/UX project planning and management, and more. I am expanding my skill set through a UX/UI Design course at "CESAE DIGITAL", in Porto.

I speak fluent English and have a strong understanding of Spanish, complementing my international mindset and openness to new opportunities.

My journey reflects a blend of adaptability, lifelong learning, and the courage to reinvent myself in pursuit of both personal and professional fulfillment.
